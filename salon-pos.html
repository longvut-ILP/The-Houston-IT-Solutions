The Hard Part, Solved
W-2 Employees and 1099 Renters,
Handled Differently

Salons run on two kinds of workers with completely different rules. Get this wrong and you risk wage violations or lost margin. The system uses the right path automatically.

Commission employee (W-2)

  • Net service revenue after card fees & product cost
  • Service & retail commission at each tech's rate
  • Minimum-wage floor and overtime checked every workweek
  • Hours tracked on a built-in time clock
  • Payroll export, generated and locked per pay period
